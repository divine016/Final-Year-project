5 Common phishing attacks

Spear Phishing

A spear phishing attack tries to trick a specific individual or group of individuals into taking actions that harm their organization or themselves.

Vishing

Vishing is short for "voice phishing," which involves scamming people over the phone, enticing them to give out sensitive information

Email Phishing

Email Phishing is any malicious email message meant to trick users into divulging private information.

Smishing

Smishing is a social engineering attack that uses fake mobile text messages to trick people into downloading malware, sharing sensitive information.

QR Phishing

Quishing (QR-phishing) is a type of phishing scam that uses QR codes to deceive users into providing their personal information or visiting hamful websites
